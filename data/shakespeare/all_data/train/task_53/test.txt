 Enter a MESSENGER Marry, I will; let them play it. Is not a comonty a Christmas gambold or a tumbling-trick? What, household stuff? Well, we'll see't. Come, madam wife, sit by my side and let the world slip;-we shall ne'er be younger.  [They sit down]  A flourish of trumpets announces the play Yes, by Saint Anne do I. A good matter, surely; comes there any more of it? 'Tis a very excellent piece of work, madam lady Would 'twere done! [They sit and mark]
