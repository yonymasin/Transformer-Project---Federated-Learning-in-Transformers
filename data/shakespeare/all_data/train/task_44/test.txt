Let me have such a bowl may hold my thanks And save me so much talking. The red wine first must rise In their fair cheeks, my lord; then we shall have 'em Talk us to silence. Yes, if I make my play. Here's to your ladyship; and pledge it, madam, For 'tis to such a thing- I told your Grace they would talk anon.  [Drum and trumpet. Chambers discharg'd]
