Pyramus enter: your cue is past; it is 'never tire.' O monstrous! O strange! We are haunted. Pray, masters! fly, masters! Help!  Exeunt all but BOTTOM and PUCK Bless thee, Bottom, bless thee! Thou art translated. Have you sent to Bottom's house? Is he come home yet? It is not possible. You have not a man in all Athens able to discharge Pyramus but he. Yea, and the best person too; and he is a very paramour for a sweet voice. Bottom! O most courageous day! O most happy hour! Let us hear, sweet Bottom.
