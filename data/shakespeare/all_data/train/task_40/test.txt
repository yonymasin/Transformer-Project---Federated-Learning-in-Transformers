Exeunt LORDS Up once again; put spirit in the French; If they miscarry, we miscarry too. They say King John, sore sick, hath left the field.  Enter MELUN, wounded It is the Count Melun. His Highness yet doth speak, and holds belief That, being brought into the open air, It would allay the burning quality Of that fell poison which assaileth him. He is more patient Than when you left him; even now he sung.
