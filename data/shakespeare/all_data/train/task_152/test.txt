I am heartily sorry that he'll be glad of this. And how mightily some other times we drown our gain in tears! The great dignity that his valour hath here acquir'd for him shall at home be encount'red with a shame as ample. They cannot be too sweet for the King's tartness. Here's his lordship now.  Enter BERTRAM How now, my lord, is't not after midnight? I will never trust a man again for keeping his sword clean; nor believe he can have everything in him by wearing his apparel neatly. This is your devoted friend, sir, the manifold linguist, and the amnipotent soldier. Why does he ask him of me? God bless you, Captain Parolles. Captain, what greeting will you to my Lord Lafeu? I am for France.
