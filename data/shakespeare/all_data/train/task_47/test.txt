You should have been respective and have kept it. Gave it a judge's clerk! No, God's my judge, The clerk will ne'er wear hair on's face that had it. Ay, if a woman live to be a man. Nor I in yours Till I again see mine. And I his clerk; therefore be well advis'd How you do leave me to mine own protection. And pardon me, my gentle Gratiano, For that same scrubbed boy, the doctor's clerk, In lieu of this, last night did lie with me. Ay, but the clerk that never means to do it, Unless he live until he be a man. Ay, and I'll give them him without a fee. There do I give to you and Jessica, From the rich Jew, a special deed of gift, After his death, of all he dies possess'd of.
