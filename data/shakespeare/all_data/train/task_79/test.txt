Thy Doll, and Helen of thy noble thoughts, Is in base durance and contagious prison; Hal'd thither By most mechanical and dirty hand. Rouse up revenge from ebon den with fell Alecto's snake, For Doll is in. Pistol speaks nought but truth. There roar'd the sea, and trumpet-clangor sounds.  Enter the KING and his train, the LORD CHIEF JUSTICE  among them The heavens thee guard and keep, most royal imp of fame! Si fortuna me tormenta, spero me contenta.  Exeunt all but PRINCE JOHN and the LORD CHIEF JUSTICE
