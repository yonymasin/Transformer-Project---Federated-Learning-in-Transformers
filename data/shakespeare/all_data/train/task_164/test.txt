And so success of mischief shall be born, And heir from heir shall hold this quarrel up Whiles England shall have generation. Go, Captain, and deliver to the army This news of peace. Let them have pay, and part. I know it will please them. Hie thee, Captain.  Exit Officer My lord, our army is dispers'd already. Like youthful steers unyok'd, they take their courses East, west, north, south; or like a school broke up, Each hurries toward his home and sporting-place.
