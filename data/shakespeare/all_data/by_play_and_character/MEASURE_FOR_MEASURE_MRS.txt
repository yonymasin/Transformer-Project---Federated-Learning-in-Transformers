OVERDONE. Well, well! there's one yonder arrested and carried
to prison was worth five thousand of you all.
OVERDONE. Marry, sir, that's Claudio, Signior Claudio.
OVERDONE. Nay, but I know 'tis so: I saw him arrested; saw him
carried away; and, which is more, within these three days his
head to be chopp'd off.
OVERDONE. I am too sure of it; and it is for getting Madam
Julietta with child.
OVERDONE. Thus, what with the war, what with the sweat, what
with the gallows, and what with poverty, I am custom-shrunk.
                           Enter POMPEY
How now! what's the news with you?
OVERDONE. Well, what has he done?
OVERDONE. But what's his offence?
OVERDONE. What! is there a maid with child by him?
OVERDONE. What proclamation, man?
OVERDONE. And what shall become of those in the city?
OVERDONE. But shall all our houses of resort in the suburbs be
pull'd down?
OVERDONE. Why, here's a change indeed in the commonwealth!
What shall become of me?
OVERDONE. What's to do here, Thomas Tapster? Let's withdraw.
OVERDONE. Good my lord, be good to me; your honour is
accounted a merciful man; good my lord.
OVERDONE. My lord, this is one Lucio's information against me.
Mistress Kate Keepdown was with child by him in the Duke's time;
he promis'd her marriage. His child is a year and a quarter old
come Philip and Jacob; I have kept it myself; and see how he goes
about to abuse me.
